What AI‑Via Does

From camera to green light — in milliseconds.

Realtime Vehicle Detection

Edge vision models detect ambulances, fire trucks, buses, and pedestrians with high accuracy — even at night or in rain.

Priority Signal Control

Dynamic phase switching clears routes for emergency vehicles and reduces average wait times for all drivers.

City‑Scale Analytics

Heatmaps, SLA tracking, and event playback help operations teams respond faster and plan better.

How it works

Simple integration with existing controllers.

1) Detect

Camera stream → on‑edge CNN/YOLO → vehicle class + lane + speed. Privacy‑preserving: no faces stored.

2) Decide

Reinforcement learning predicts optimal phase changes to minimize delay and prioritize emergencies.

3) Act

Signal controller receives commands via secure API/IO to switch to green corridors.

4) Learn

Continuous feedback loop updates policies per intersection and time of day.
